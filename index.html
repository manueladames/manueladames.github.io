About

I'm a QA Automation Engineer with over 7 years of experience building and implementing end-to-end testing frameworks that scale. My expertise lies in designing robust automation architectures using tools like Cypress, Katalon Studio, Selenium, and Playwright.

Currently, I'm a Senior QA Automation Engineer at TechCorp Solutions, where I lead the quality engineering initiatives for a platform serving millions of users. I focus on building CI/CD-integrated testing pipelines, implementing visual regression testing, and establishing quality metrics that drive product decisions.

In my past roles, I've had the opportunity to transform manual testing processes into fully automated workflows, reducing regression testing time by up to 80%. I'm passionate about test-driven development, behavior-driven development, and creating testing cultures that empower development teams.

When I'm not debugging flaky tests or optimizing test suites, you'll find me contributing to open-source testing tools, writing technical blogs about QA best practices, or mentoring aspiring automation engineers.
